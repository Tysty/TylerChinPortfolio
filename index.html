My name is Tyler Chin. I live in Sunnyvale California. I like to play video games and watch anime in my free time. I also enjoy coding video games and applications. I join game jams and hackathons to hone my programming skills. My goal is to become a video game programmer for a big company

I am currently a software programmer in my school's robotics team, "The King's Academy". The name of our team is "Midnight Madness". I collaborate with the hardware and electrical team in order to determine how to code the functionality for the robot. For programmming the autonomous for our robot, I use OpenCV, a image detection library in order for the robot to decide where it needs to park. I also use a complex coding algorithm called odometry where we put 3 wheels perpendicular from each other and track their rotations. I then plug it into a formula to determine where the robot is in an xy plane. This ensures that the robot will move accurately with little to no mistakes. It is fun to see the final product of everyone's hard work. It is even more exciting to see our robot compete against other robots from different teams in our region.
